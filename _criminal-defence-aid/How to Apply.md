<h3>Online Applications</h3>
<p>Apply online at <a href="https://go.gov.sg/applypdo" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">go.gov.sg/applypdo</a>.</p>
<p>If you encounter difficulties and need help with the completion of the
online application form, please refer to our application guide for more
information (click <a href="/files/Online%20application%20guide.pdf" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">here</a>).</p>

<h4>Supporting documents required</h4>
<p>You will need to submit the following supporting documents:</p>
<p><strong>Court documents</strong>
</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Bail bond (or bailorâ€™s confirmation, whichever applicable)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Cautioned statement(s)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Charge sheet(s)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Latest court mention slip</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Any other document from the court, or medical reports where applicable</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Financial documents</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Latest bank statement(s) or bank account passbook(s)</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>(Please provide bank statement(s) from the most recent calendar month,
with your full name and bank account number clearly reflected. For bank
passbook(s), please provide the first page bearing the account holder's
name and account number, and the last updated page. All transactions from
the most recent month before the application date <u>must</u> be clearly
reflected)</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Payslips for the last 12 months (if employed without CPF contributions)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Any other supporting documents to show employment and income (if self-employed)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Latest Central Depository (CDP) account statement (if relevant)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Updated/Latest statements of investment products (such as fixed deposits,
unit trusts, Singapore Saving Bonds and other investment products)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Identity card (if applying in-person at MinLaw Services Centre or State
Courts HELP Centre)</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p>You can refer to our supporting documents checklist to confirm that you
have prepared the required documents.</p>
<h3>Cannot find your charge sheet?</h3>
<p>Submitting a copy of your charge sheet is mandatory. If you do not have
a copy of your charge sheet or other court documents, you can refer to
the guide on how to download from ICMS ( click <a href="/files/Guide%20on%20how%20to%20download%20charge%20sheet%20for%20ICMS.pdf" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">here</a>)</p>
